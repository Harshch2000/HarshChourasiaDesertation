About StudyTube

StudyTube is an educational platform founded by Harsh Chourasia as part of a college dissertation project. The vision behind StudyTube is to help students focus on learning by providing curated study videos from trusted educational channels in a clean and distraction-free environment. This platform aims to make studying more organized, efficient, and accessible for students across different subjects and academic levels.
